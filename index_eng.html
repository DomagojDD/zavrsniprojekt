About Me

My name is Domagoj Dugi and I'm 22 and live in Varaždin, Croatia. I'm photographer, videographer, web designer and more or less everything related to multimedia. I always try to combine my knowledge and skills to make my projects better and more successful.

When I enrolled in high school for a media technician I'm gained a lot of knowledge and experience about photography, video and television. Also, I participated in projects Comenius and ACES for which ones I was in Turkey and FYR Macedonia where I met other cultures and gained new acquaintances.

After that, I enrolled at the University North on the department of Multimedia, design and application which I'm still attending. Here I have further improved my knowledge and gained some new skills such as design and programming.

My achievements:

2017 – My two photos at the exhibition of street photography for the MOP festival.

2014 - My two photos published in the tourist guide for children „Discover Croatia – A Children's Guide“.

2013 - My photo has entered the narrow circle of photo contest "Hunting, fishing, nature and tourism" in the newspaper Regionalni tjednik.
